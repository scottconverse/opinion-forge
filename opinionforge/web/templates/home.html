{# ================================================================ Stance Slider ================================================================ #}
Stance ? 0 — balanced
The stance slider controls the argumentative direction of the piece on a scale from -100 (strongly equity-focused) to +100 (strongly liberty-focused). A value of 0 produces a balanced perspective. The further from center, the more the argument leans toward one side.
Range: -100 (equity-focused) to +100 (liberty-focused). 0 = balanced.
{# ================================================================ Intensity Slider ================================================================ #}
Intensity ? 0.5 — moderate
Intensity controls the rhetorical heat of the writing. At 0.0, the prose is measured and even-handed. At 1.0, the writing uses maximum conviction with strong rhetorical devices and forceful language.
Range: 0.0 (measured) to 1.0 (maximum conviction).
