About OpinionForge

An editorial craft engine for AI-assisted opinion writing

What It Is

OpinionForge is an editorial craft engine that generates publication-ready opinion pieces — op-eds, columns, and long-form opinion content — with precise rhetorical control. It is built around 12 distinct rhetorical modes that define argumentative approach, prose style, and rhetorical devices.

This is a tool for improving your own rhetoric — not for imitating anyone else's. Choose a mode based on what the argument demands, not who you want to sound like.

Version: 1.0.0

The 12 Rhetorical Modes

Each mode is a self-contained rhetorical system with its own prose patterns, argument structure, and signature devices. Modes are organized into four categories:

  • Confrontational — Polemical, Populist, Provocative. Direct, forceful, takes-a-side rhetoric.
  • Investigative — Forensic, Data-Driven. Evidence-first, builds a documented case.
  • Deliberative — Analytical, Dialectical, Measured. Weighs arguments carefully, arrives at earned conclusions.
  • Literary — Satirical, Oratorical, Narrative, Aphoristic. Craft-forward, uses literary techniques to advance arguments.

You can use a single mode, or blend two modes together with adjustable weights to create hybrid rhetorical approaches. Browse all modes.

Stance & Intensity Controls

OpinionForge separates what rhetorical approach to use from how to position the argument and how forcefully to argue it:

  • Stance (-100 to +100) — Argumentative emphasis direction. Ranges from strongly equity-focused (-100) through balanced (0) to strongly liberty-focused (+100). These are policy-orientation labels, not political party affiliations.
  • Intensity (0.0 to 1.0) — Rhetorical heat. 0.0 produces measured, deliberative prose. 1.0 produces writing at maximum conviction. The mode's rhetorical character stays the same; only the forcefulness changes.

Disclaimer Policy

Every piece generated by OpinionForge includes the following mandatory disclaimer:

This piece was generated with AI-assisted rhetorical controls. It is original content and is not written by, endorsed by, or affiliated with any real person.

This disclaimer is included in every generated piece and cannot be removed. It appears in the output regardless of export format. This policy exists because OpinionForge generates original AI content with a defined rhetorical character. The disclaimer ensures readers understand what they are reading. It is not negotiable.

How It Works

When you submit a topic, OpinionForge:

  1. Analyzes the topic and prepares research context
  2. Blends the selected rhetorical mode(s) with your stance and intensity settings
  3. Generates an original opinion piece using AI with the configured rhetorical controls
  4. Screens the output for originality to ensure it does not reproduce existing content
  5. Delivers the finished piece with the mandatory disclaimer attached
