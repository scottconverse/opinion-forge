Quick Tour

Here's a quick overview of the main interface features.

Mode Selector

Choose from 12 rhetorical modes like Analytical, Polemical, Satirical, and more. Select two to create a custom blend.

Stance Slider

Control the argumentative direction from equity-focused to liberty-focused, or keep it balanced at centre.

Intensity Slider

Adjust rhetorical heat from measured and deliberative to maximum conviction and force.

Generate Button

Enter a topic and hit Generate. OpinionForge researches, writes, and screens for originality automatically.
